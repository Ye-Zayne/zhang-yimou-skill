# 01 · 作品阶段与风格地图

This is a craft map, not a definitive filmography. Verify exact years, credits, awards, latest releases, and current projects before making factual claims.

## 阶段 1：摄影意识与第五代起点

Core transferable ideas:

- Image before exposition: the first strong image should already contain social pressure.
- Bodies and landscape are not background; they carry fate, labor, desire, and violence.
- Color can be primitive, physical, and moral rather than merely decorative.

Short-drama translation:

- Start from one visual contradiction: festive red over humiliation, white snow over murder, golden banquet over debt.
- Let the environment impose behavior: mud road slows a pursuit, courtyard exposes gossip, window counter denies emotion.

## 阶段 2：乡土寓言与家族秩序

Core transferable ideas:

- Family, village, clan, marriage, ritual, fertility, shame, and gossip become visible systems.
- Women are often framed inside rules, but strong scenes give them tactical agency.
- Repetition makes oppression legible: lighting, eating, waiting, bowing, washing, carrying.

Short-drama translation:

- Use family meal, wedding ceremony, funeral queue, ancestral hall, village committee, or neighborhood stairwell.
- A single repeated action should change meaning across the scene.
- Make the private wound public through ritual.

## 阶段 3：现实主义小人物

Core transferable ideas:

- A small goal can become a moral engine if repeated against institutional indifference.
- Procedures, stamps, forms, queues, transportation, and waiting can be more dramatic than shouting.
- Amateur texture and ordinary locations can increase credibility.

Short-drama translation:

- Hospital, police station, school office, labor bureau, property management desk, delivery station, factory gate.
- The protagonist's goal should be concrete: get a stamp, find a witness, retrieve a file, deliver money, prove attendance.
- Keep dialogue plain and let repetition create pressure.

## 阶段 4：武侠、权力空间与色彩编码

Core transferable ideas:

- Each color can represent a version of truth, a political force, or a remembered emotion.
- Bodies become graphic forms: line, circle, wall, procession, wave.
- Symmetry can feel beautiful and authoritarian at the same time.

Short-drama translation:

- Use company hierarchy, rich family banquet, school ranking board, influencer studio, courtroom mediation, or public ceremony.
- Assign color to power blocs: management black, family gold, protagonist white, evidence blue.
- Rehearse blocking: who enters center, who cannot cross a threshold, who is reflected in glass.

## 阶段 5：极简、水墨、替身与身份

Core transferable ideas:

- Limiting the palette can sharpen moral ambiguity.
- Doubles, shadows, screens, curtains, reflections, and masks can externalize identity conflict.
- Softness and hardness can reverse: water can be weapon, cloth can be barrier, silence can be attack.

Short-drama translation:

- Suitable for:替嫁, 替身, 失忆, 伪证, hidden recording, "one room truth duel".
- Keep props few and precise: umbrella, curtain, mirror, document, voice recorder.
- Let the image carry the deception before dialogue reveals it.

## 阶段 6：类型片压力锅

Core transferable ideas:

- Compressed time and sealed spaces create automatic escalation.
- Comedy can coexist with danger if every joke exposes a system.
- Final reversal should change moral responsibility, not only plot information.

Short-drama translation:

- One night, one inspection, one office floor, one wedding banquet, one live-stream room, one hospital corridor.
- Every 10-15 seconds: new witness, new object, new lie, new rule, new silence.
- Use ensemble roles: controller, witness, clown, hidden victim, false helper, real judge.

## 阶段 7：国家级仪式与集体图像

Core transferable ideas:

- One image can carry a national, historical, or collective thesis.
- Collective movement should be readable as order, memory, or transformation.
- Technology and spectacle are effective only when symbolic meaning is clear.

Short-drama translation:

- Low budget does not block ritual image-making: a row of desks, ten identical uniforms, phone lights, umbrellas, elevator doors, red seals.
- Use pattern rather than crowd size.
- A clean symbolic image often beats a busy expensive image.
